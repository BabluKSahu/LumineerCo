Our Story

LumineerCo was founded with a simple yet powerful vision: to create a seamless digital marketplace that bridges the gap between customers seeking quality services and local professionals looking to grow their businesses.

In today's fast-paced world, finding reliable service providers can be challenging, while talented local professionals often struggle to reach their potential customers. LumineerCo solves both problems through our innovative platform.

By booking a service through LumineerCo, you agree to provide accurate information and acknowledge that:

  • Service requests are subject to provider availability
  • LumineerCo acts as an intermediary platform
  • Final service terms are between you and the service provider
  • We reserve the right to refuse service at our discretion

2. Pricing & Payments

  • Quoted prices are estimates and may vary based on actual requirements
  • Additional charges may apply for extra work not included in initial quote
  • Payments must be made as per agreed terms with the service provider
  • LumineerCo may charge a platform fee for certain services

3. Cancellation & Refunds

  • Cancellations within 24 hours of scheduled service may incur charges
  • Refund eligibility depends on service provider's cancellation policy
  • No-shows will be charged 50% of the quoted amount
  • Refunds processed within 7-10 business days if approved

4. Service Quality & Disputes

  • LumineerCo verifies providers but doesn't guarantee specific outcomes
  • Service issues must be reported within 24 hours of completion
  • We mediate disputes but final resolution rests with involved parties
  • Repeat complaints may lead to provider suspension from platform

5. Customer Responsibilities

  • Provide accurate service requirements and access to premises
  • Maintain respectful communication with service providers
  • Ensure safe working conditions for service personnel
  • Clear payment obligations as per agreed terms
